Programmable Logic Controller training course [Runs on Friday/Saturday, 4 weeks, 10 hours] Total costs: £500


The candidates required no prior knowledge and experience as we start from scratch. The PLC training course aims to enable keen engineering apprentices, electricians and young engineers to familiarise with basic operations of PLC in industry and their basic troubleshooting.

Course Contents:

  • Siemens step S7-1200/1500 programming
  • Basics of PLC
  • Inputs/outputs interfacing of PLCs
  • Standards and communication protocols for PLCs
  • Timer and counter programming for PLCs
  • Basic fault finding and troubleshooting of PLCs
  • Automation, RTU and SCADA basics with PLCs

Instrumentation and process control course [Runs on Friday/Saturday, 6 weeks, 15 hours] Total costs: £625


The aim of this course is to enable electrician, apprentices, electrical, maintenance and electronic engineer to carry out daily activities and scheduled maintenance on sensors, transducers, actuators and process control devices used in industrial systems. Both online and in-person deliveries are available.

Course Contents:

  • Introduction to transducer, sensors and actuators.
  • Measurement and testing of inductive/capacitive proximity switches, temperature, pressure, position and level detector sensors.
  • Introduction to block diagram of industrial systems.
  • Introduction to PID control techniques for an industrial system.
  • Designing and testing of an instrumentation amplifier for industrial systems on printed circuit board
  • Basic soldering skills
  • Modelling instruments for industrial systems (participants will work on 3 industrial systems)
  • Basic MATLAB and LTSpice circuits/systems to demonstrate course contents
